🟩 Guaranteed Pests

These pests are included in your Greenix subscription and treated proactively during quarterly visits.

  • 🕷️ Spiders
  • 🐜 Ants
  • 🪳 Cockroach (non-German)
  • 🦗 Crickets
  • 🐌 Pill Bugs

Script: “These are part of our guaranteed list—we treat them automatically as part of your regular service.”

🛎️ Courtesy Pests

These pests are not covered by our guarantee, but can be treated upon customer request. Always document communication in appointment notes.

  • 🧫 Clover Mites
  • 🦟 Chiggers
  • 🪰 Stink Bugs
  • 🐞 Japanese Beetles
  • 🐝 Carpenter Bees (≤10 holes)
  • 🐍 Snakes

Script: “We treat those pests on request! They're part of our courtesy category—not covered by guarantee, but we help during the visit.”
